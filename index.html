How System Works

Simple Steps to transform university hostel management experience

Student Guide

  • Login with your ID and password
  • Check-in and check-out using Fingerprint scanner
  • View your room allocation and roommate details

  • Monitor student attendance and late returns

  • Generate attendance and occupancy reports
  • Configure system settings

Admin Guide

  • Manage user accounts and access permissions
  • Configure system-wide settings and policies
  • Monitor student performance and usage
  • Generate comprehensive system reports
  • Manage hostel facilities and resources
